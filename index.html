Background

In October 2018, I joined the Institute for Datability Science (Osaka University) as a postdoc. I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science from Aston University, United Kingdom, in March 2019. In my thesis, I explored different modalities of visual retrieval, including image retrieval, image-video retrieval, and image-text retrieval. Before that, I worked for 2 years at GATV (Universidad Politecnica de Madrid) in a parking optimization project. I obtained my Telecom Engineering degree from Universitat Polictecnica de Catalunya, Spain, back in 2012.
